# Previous goal (2026-07-15, re-confirmed 2026-07-16)

**Set:** 2026-07-15 (re-confirmed and expanded via a second HelmControl New Goal intake on 2026-07-16)
**Status:** in progress (blocked entirely on user-side actions — see Notes)

A Trello-style kanban app for a defined organization (not public), backed by Supabase for auth/database. Multi-user with accounts, permissions, and invites to boards by username or email. Boards support fully customizable backgrounds (color wheel or user-uploaded photo) on both a board level and adjustable per-member. Cards support attachments of any file type (images, scripts, zips, links) with image previews shown directly on the card face — even with no card selected — using the most recently added image as the cover, with an explicit "make cover" option to override. Cards have a visual-only completion checkmark (no backend logic beyond saving the state) that is synced so all members see when someone marks a task complete in real time. Cards show a "created at" timestamp. An in-app-only notification section covers board invites and other events (like task completions). A calendar view shows cards by due date, offers a subscribable feed link for Google Calendar, and also supports full two-way OAuth sync with each member's individual Google account (Google Cloud OAuth app in testing mode, org members added as test users — no public verification needed). Recent bug fixes are also in scope: drag-and-drop cards disappearing mid-drag, and a white-rectangle rendering glitch on far horizontal scroll.

## Done when
- Multi-user accounts and permissions work on top of Supabase auth: board creators can invite others by username or email, and non-owner members are correctly gated from owner-only actions.
- Boards support fully customizable backgrounds via both a color picker and an uploaded photo, at the board level.
- Board backgrounds are also individually adjustable per-member (a member can override the board's background for their own view without changing what other members see).
- Cards accept attachments of any file type plus links; image attachments show an inline thumbnail preview.
- An in-app notification section surfaces board invites and other board events, including task completions (no email delivery).
- Cards display their creation timestamp.
- Card fronts show a cover image without opening the card — defaulting to the most recently added image attachment, with a manual "make cover" override that persists.
- Cards have a visual-only completion checkmark on the card front that toggles and persists without driving any other backend behavior (no filtering/automation tied to it), and that update is synced live to every other member viewing the board (real-time, not just on reload).
- Dragging a card between lists never makes it disappear mid-drag; scrolling the board as far right as it goes never reveals a stray white (or any other non-background-colored) rectangle.
- Cards have a start-date field (optionally a plain deadline with no end time, or a real meeting with an end time) and a calendar view that shows cards laid out by date, with a time range shown for meeting-style cards.
- The calendar view offers a subscribable feed link (ICS) that an external calendar app (e.g. Google Calendar's "From URL" import) can subscribe to for read-only updates.
- Each user can connect their own Google account via OAuth and get full two-way sync between their cards' dates and their Google Calendar: card date changes propagate to Google Calendar as either an all-day event (deadline-style) or a timed event with a real start/end (meeting-style), and changes made on the Google Calendar side propagate back onto the card, correctly distinguishing all-day from timed events.
- The Google OAuth consent screen runs in "testing" publish status with organization members added as test users — no public verification flow required.
- All of the above verified live in a real browser against the live Supabase backend (and a real connected Google account for the calendar sync), not just via unit/type checks.
